The Ultimate Guide to Layering

December 1, 2025 â€¢ Style Guide

Layering is more than just piling on clothes to stay warm; it's an art form that adds depth, texture, and versatility to your outfit. Whether you're navigating the fluctuating temperatures of spring or building a cozy fortress against winter's chill, efficient layering is a skill every stylish individual should master.

The Rule of Three

The most effective layering strategy typically involves three key layers:

  • Base Layer: This sits against your skin. Opt for breathable fabrics like our organic cotton tees that wick moisture and keep you comfortable.
  • Middle Layer: This provides insulation. Think cardigans, light sweaters, or denim jackets.
  • Outer Layer: The final shield against the elements. A trench coat, leather jacket, or heavy wool coat completes the look.

Mixing Textures

Don't be afraid to mix materials. Pair a smooth cotton tee with a chunky knit cardigan and a sleek leather jacket. The contrast creates visual interest and makes your outfit look intentional and curated.

Remember, the goal is to be able to remove layers as the day warms up without sacrificing your style. Each layer should look good on its own.
