How to Make Your Tees Last Forever

November 25, 2025 â€¢ Care Tips

We've all been there: you buy the perfect white tee, and three washes later, it's a twisted, shrunken shadow of its former self. But it doesn't have to be this way. With a little know-how, your Urban Threads essentials can look brand new for years.

Wash Less, Wash Cold

Over-washing is the number one enemy of clothing longevity. Unless it's noticeably dirty, try airing your tee out. When you do wash, always choose a cold cycle (30Â°C or lower). Hot water breaks down fibers and leads to shrinkage.

Skip the Dryer

Tumble dryers are intense. The high heat and tumbling action can damage the fabric and fade colors. Instead, gently reshape your damp tee and lay it flat to dry. Hanging it while wet can sometimes stretch the neck, so flat drying is king.

Store Properly

Fold your tees; don't hang them. Gravity can pull at the shoulders of a hanger-hung t-shirt, causing misshapen bumps. A neatly folded stack in your drawer is the best way to keep them crisp and ready to wear.
