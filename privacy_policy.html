Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025


1. Introduction

Welcome to Urban Threads. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.


2. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together follows:

  • Identity Data: includes first name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: includes billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers.
  • Technical Data: includes internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform.
  • Usage Data: includes information about how you use our website, products and services.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and also allows us to improve our site.

We use a consent management platform to allow you to control which cookies are set. You can adjust your preferences at any time.


4. Google Analytics & Tracking

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors engage with our site. If you deny consent for analytics storage, your data will not be included in our analytics reports. We have implemented Google Consent Mode v2 to respect your privacy choices.


5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: privacy@urbanthreads.com
